Wicker chair

September 14th 2021

Ficks Reed Co wicker chair

I bought this broken off of a resale site for a client decorating a bakery but they ended up passing on this piece because of the fragility of wicker. I cut out the entirety of the seat and wove strips of upholstery fabric through the remaining structure to make a new seat. I stapled the corner pieces to ensure the material would not sag or move, removed loose nails and sanded / sealed sharp edges. I also glued loose pieces of decorative wicker wrappings.
